Pfynn Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What We Collect

Pfynn collects the following data:

  • Account information: Email address for login
  • Financial data: Bank balances and credit card information via Plaid (stored securely, never shared)
  • Usage analytics: App opens, feature usage (opt-out available)

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the weekly review service
  • Improve app functionality (anonymous analytics)
  • Send weekly reminders (if enabled)

Third-Party Services

  • Plaid: Secure bank connections
  • Supabase: Data storage
  • PostHog: Anonymous analytics

Your Rights

  • Opt-out of analytics in Settings
  • Delete your account and all data at any time
  • Contact us for data export requests
